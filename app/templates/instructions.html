Getting Started

Click View PDF to see the full text of the opinion.
Click View Oyez to see Oyez info on the referenced case, pulling from their API to get the latest information. Brief AI summarization is included for reference.
Use the Dark Mode toggle in the navbar for a night-friendly view.
Click Search to pull up cases with a string of interest.

Corpus Tools

  • Click KWIC to see a Keyword in Context view, which shows the search term in context with surrounding text. You can specify number of characters to the left and right.
  • Use Left/Right Char Filters to control how many characters appear on each side of your search term in KWIC view, expanding your context as needed.
    Click Collocates to find words that frequently appear near your search term in the corpus.
    In Collocates view:
    • Use the Part of Speech filter to focus on specific word types (nouns, verbs, etc.)
    • Adjust Window Size to control how many words around your search term are analyzed for collocations
    • Use Results Size to limit how many collocates are displayed in your results
